Lehigh University PA 100 Index Team

Based on an original idea by Professor Nandkumar Nayar in 2020, flanked by industry expert Alex Matturri and supported by Lehigh's Master in Financial Engineering (MFE) program and its manager Patrick Zoro, The Lehigh University PA 100 Index is constructed, maintained, and updated by a team of Lehigh University Students. This was originally a capstone project of the universityâ€™s MS in Financial Engineering program. In addition it is a Lehigh University tool aimed at becoming a reference of performance for companies operating in Pennsylvania. The index has seen involvement of Lehigh students from a diverse range of disciplines. This project is made possible by MFE students and the contribution of multiple offices and programs at Lehigh University.
